Stem Innovators Benefit, About program

The STEM Innovators curriculum is designed to introduce children to the world of programming and technology through the use of Micro:bit. Kids will learn basic programming concepts, explore Micro:bit hardware, and develop creative projects using this platform. This curriculum aims to increase students' understanding of computing, creativity, and problem-solving skills.
